Common Chromebook Issues

Chromebook Won’t Turn On

  • Charge for 15 minutes
  • Hold power for 10 seconds
  • Try another charger
  • Submit a tech request if needed

Student Can’t Log In

  • Check username carefully
  • Turn off Caps Lock
  • Restart Chromebook
  • Contact school tech support

Wi-Fi Problems

  • Restart Chromebook
  • Reconnect to Wi-Fi
  • Restart home router
  • Move closer to router

Chromebook Expectations

  • Charge devices nightly
  • Handle devices carefully
  • Report damage immediately
  • Use school accounts appropriately
